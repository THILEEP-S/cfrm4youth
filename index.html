About Us

How CFRM formed

On 2018 February, four Prayerful Catholic Youth in chennai, decided to form a Catholic Gospel Band, But god had another plan. This has became a Youth movement. A movement for Youth Group to lead the Youth life towards God according to Catholic Faith! Then, the group got the name as CATHOLIC FAITH REVIVAL MOVEMENT on March 2018 and started the activities under the guidance of Fr.Arulmani. CFRM led their first Youth gathering on April, 2018 at Infant Jesus Chruch, Thendral nagar - Chennai. Many faithful participated and were benefitted. This led to an amazing decision of conducting Youth Retreat's on the fourth sunday of every month, in different parishes in and around Chennai. Now, CFRM has officially 7 Organisers, 12 Core team members and 200 registered members across continents!

Prayer is an impotant part of life. As Catholics, we are called to pray daily in the way of our Lord Jesus Christ taught us. CFRM is an intiative that helps Catholics to Renew the relationship with God. CFRM is a congregation under the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Madras, where we acknowledge that we are the children of God and Brothers and Sisters in Christ called to live in Faith and Prayer.

Several retreats were organized by CFRM since its inception The main focus of the retreats is to revive the draimed faith and provide healing to people. The retreats start with Praise and Worship, followed by message and blessings by Father, and conclude with Eucharistic Adoration. Stay-in retreats were conducted in Retreat Centers, while One-day retreats were conducted in Parish churchs.

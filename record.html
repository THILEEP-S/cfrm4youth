What we done

Catechism classes for Students

During the second wave of the COVID-19 pandemic, CFRM organized online Catechism classes via Google meet, as churches wore closed.
The Catechism Classes went on effectively with lots of fun-based learning activities. The participants were enthusiastic about learning about God and the Church..

CFRM Competitions

CFRM Organized Don Bosco Quiz competitions for Catechism Kids, on account of St. Don Bosco's Feast, on 30-January-2022. The Students participated actively and the winners were gifted certificates and prizes through post.

Food Relief

During the November 2021 rains and flood, CFRM Team had distributed food packets to the needy in Perambur area.

Intercessions

Priests from different parishes and retreat centers were invited to preside over every intercession. Special messages and blessings were given to the public by the priests.
Participants from the general public were alloted various prayer roles. The lockdown due to the pandemic has led to lots of difficulties and changes in people's routines.
As churches were closed from Friday through Sunday, CFRM Conducted online Prayer sessions every Friday and Sunday.

Month of Holy Rosary

CFRM conducted an Online Rosay Prayer service from October 1st to October 31st via Google Meet.
Rosary REcitation was followed by a short meditation on our Lady's Apparition.

CFRM ACTIVITIES

Several retreats were organized by CFRM since its inception The main focus of the retreats is to revive the draimed faith and provide healing to people. The retreats start with Praise and Worship, followed by message and blessings by Father, and conclude with Eucharistic Adoration.
Stay-in retreats were conducted in Retreat Centers, while One-day retreats were conducted in Parish churchs.

CFRM Monthly

To rekindle catholic faith and spread scripture awareness among public, daily posts and videos are being uploaded in CFRM's Instagram and Facebook handles and CFRM Whatsapp group. The topics include:
